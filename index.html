About Me

I'm Sam, a software engineer with experience in Ruby on Rails, Express, JavaScript, React, Redux, HTML, CSS, and a background in mechanical engineering. My interest in programming comes from my love of problem solving, and the opportunity to make a positive impact on the world! I enjoy collaboration on complex problems and being able to see different perspectives/approaches to solutions.

I'm always seeking new challenges and opportunities to expand my skill set and grow as a software engineer!

When not developing apps, I enjoy being in nature, landscape photography, competitive powerlifting, and volunteering my time to installing residential solar PV systems for low income households. I hope to connect with you soon!

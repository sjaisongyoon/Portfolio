About Me

Hi! I'm Sam, a Bay Area native and software engineer with experience in Ruby on Rails, Express, JavaScript, React, Redux, HTML, CSS. After graduating from UC Berkeley with a degree in mechanical engineering, I've dedicated the last five years to designing systems to provide cost-effective access to renewable energy solutions.

My passion for programming comes from my love of problem solving and creating user-friendly products that seamlessly integrate hardware and software. Through my experience as a product manager and engineer, I've learned to seek out various solutions to collaborate on complex problems. I'm always excited to take on new opportunities to expand my skill set and grow as a software engineer!

When not developing apps, I enjoy backpacking, landscape photography, competitive powerlifting, and volunteering my time installing residential solar PV systems for low income households. Thanks for stopping by, and I hope to connect with you soon!

In the meantime, feel free to explore my existing projects as well as my résumé.
